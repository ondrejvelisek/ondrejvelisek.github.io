What is Torrta?

👁️

She is a vision of a future of frontend ecosystem. What one frontend app developer dreams of.

🙏🏻

She is one app developer wish on how the frontend ecosystem may work so he would be happier in his work.

🔤

She is an acronym for title of my blog post “The one react rule them all” where she was born.

😫

She is a result of frustration while I was using React, NextJs, Remix, Gatsby for a while.

📨

She is a message to framework maintainers (who I admire for their skills) that there are obstackles in using their APIs.

🙋🏾

She is a place to get feedback from people smarter than me for my thoughts, so I can learn.

🌊

She is a pool of thoughts where readers (like you) could swim and imaginate the future with me.

📝

She is an API specification which helps me to organize those thoughts.

👨🏻‍💻

She a buggy proof of concept which makes me believe it is not garbage and gives me enough courage to publish here.
